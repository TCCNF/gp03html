Non-Fungible Token (NFT)

What is NFT?

NFT is a form of crowdsourcing used to help project parties. It is a data element on the blockchain electronic ledger as well. A unique piece of digital data may be represented by each token, which can also act as an electronic certification or proof of ownership for virtual goods.



Non-fungible tokens can represent digital assets like pictures, artwork, audio, movies, in-game projects, or other types of creative effort because of their non-fungibility.The tokens used to represent the works are completely trackable on the underlying blockchain, giving purchasers evidence of ownership even though the works t hemselves are indefinitely reproducible.
